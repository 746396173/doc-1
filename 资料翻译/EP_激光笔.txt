-----------绿光 功率不足 解释----------------------------------

The 200MW small green laser, do have infrared conversion rate is not high issue;
Infrared leak, infrared laser 1064nm and 808nm are the fundamental frequency light produces 532nm laser, 
we call the conversion efficiency, it refers to the efficiency of 808nm to 1064nm to 532nm, 
if the residual amount of 808nm and 1064nm output is large, 
then participate in the excitation of the 808nm and 1064nm will certainly less frequency.
